User Survey

This survey form is a tongue-in-cheek survey intended for owners of the Samsung Galaxy Note 7 (the phone that was prone to exploding). It is intended to somewhat look like the phone. I wanted my first front-end project to have some character and be a bit more challenging. It is not mobile responsive, ironically enough. The skeumorphism is limited, but heartfelt.

Zoomtunes

This landing page is for a concept product called Zoomtunes, a practice aid for musicians. This project involved a substantial amount of non-coding work: I created the concept, designed the logo, sourced the background from Canva, and went through an iterative wire-framing process. My goal was to use color, shape, and space to create a sense of friendliness and kinetic energy.
